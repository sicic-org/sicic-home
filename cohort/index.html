Cohort¶

Overview¶

Our cohorts focus on building real AI applications. Participants work on projects involving AI agents, automation, and practical use cases.

Founding Cohort¶

The founding cohort is the first membership group inside Super Individuals. These early members are building in public, shipping real work, and establishing the tone for what the community becomes.

Projects are built within a limited timeframe. The focus is on execution, not perfection.
