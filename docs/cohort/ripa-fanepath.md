## What It Is

Lucile's project is a public ZeroClaw lab connected to her work at FanePath AI.

The project sits at the intersection of brand strategy and practical AI systems: how brands are represented, discovered, and understood as AI tools become part of search, recommendation, and customer decision-making.

## Why Lucile Built This

AI visibility is becoming an operational question for brands, not only a marketing question.

Lucile's lab gives that problem a practical build surface: a place to document agent workflows, test AI-assisted brand analysis, and turn strategic questions into repeatable systems.

## Project Direction

The live demo is positioned as a working ZeroClaw agent lab. It can support:

- brand visibility analysis
- AI-mediated search and discovery workflows
- structured research outputs for brand positioning
- repeatable client-facing AI solution patterns

## Why It Matters

This is a useful founding cohort example because it shows how agent infrastructure can support a commercial strategy problem.

Instead of treating AI visibility as an abstract trend, the project turns it into something that can be built, tested, documented, and improved.
