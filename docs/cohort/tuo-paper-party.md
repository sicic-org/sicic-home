## What It Does

Paper Party is designed around a simple research workflow:

- upload a research paper in PDF form
- discover the surrounding academic conversation
- join a discussion table and debate with the literature

## Core Idea

The live demo frames academic reading as something more social and navigable. Instead of treating a paper as an isolated document, the product aims to help users see the surrounding conversation and step into it.

## Published Flow

The current live page presents the project in three stages:

### Upload

Drop a research paper in PDF format.

### Discover

AI maps the literature landscape around the paper.

### Dialogue

Users join tables and debate with the literature.

## Why It Matters

This is a strong founding cohort example because it takes a familiar knowledge task, reading a paper, and reframes it as a structured interactive workflow. The project is not only about summarising content. It is about helping users move from solitary reading to contextualised academic conversation.

## Closing Thought

Paper Party points toward a more collaborative research experience: upload the paper, see the wider discussion, and enter the conversation with more context than a standalone PDF can provide.
