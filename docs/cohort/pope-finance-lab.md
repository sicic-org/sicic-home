## What It Is

Rachel's project is a public ZeroClaw lab connected to finance leadership, sovereign finance, and portfolio-level decision work.

The lab gives finance questions a practical build surface: a place to test agent workflows, document how the system is structured, and turn high-level analysis into reusable outputs.

## Why Rachel Built This

Finance leadership often depends on interpreting uncertainty, structuring trade-offs, and communicating clearly across strategic and operational audiences.

Rachel's lab uses agent infrastructure to support that kind of thinking: not as a black-box answer machine, but as a repeatable environment for analysis, documentation, and decision support.

## Project Direction

The live demo is positioned as a working ZeroClaw agent lab. It can support:

- structured finance analysis
- portfolio and sovereign finance research workflows
- decision-support briefs and reusable notes
- finance leadership knowledge capture

## Why It Matters

This is a useful founding cohort example because it shows how owned AI systems can support senior finance work.

The value is not only automation. It is the ability to turn judgment-heavy workflows into documented, repeatable systems that can improve over time.
