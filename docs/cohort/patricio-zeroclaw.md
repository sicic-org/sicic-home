## Why Mafalda Built This

In the AI era, Mafalda wants to own assets, not just use tools.

This lab documents her agent architecture, methodology, and everything that broke along the way.

## Current Lead Agent

### Nova

Nova is Mafalda's Content Intelligence Officer.

What Nova does:

- monitors YouTube business intelligence continuously
- converts transcripts into decision briefs
- filters low-ROI content before it reaches her

## Why It Matters

This is one of the early public outputs from the founding cohort. It shows a clear move from generic AI tool usage toward owning a focused agent system with a defined operational role.
