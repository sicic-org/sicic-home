# Community

We are building a curated community of builders.

## Membership

Members get:

- access to community discussions
- invitations to events
- opportunities to collaborate on projects

## Invitation-Only Membership

Membership is not open for public payment.
It is offered only after a participant completes and passes the intro workshop, followed by a final review of results.

If invited, the participant receives a private payment link by email.
