# Partners

## Partnership Direction

Super Individuals CIC is designed to work with institutions, communities, alumni networks, and mission-aligned organizations that want to help domain experts build practical, domain-specific AI capability.

## Current Context

Our first workshop was framed in an Oxford Saïd alumni context, giving the community an early home among thoughtful professionals.

## Future Partners

We are open to collaborations with:

- alumni communities
- universities and executive education programs
- innovation labs and public-interest organizations
- founder networks and professional associations
- media, research, and cultural institutions

## Partnership Formats

- private workshop cohorts
- invited talks and salon events
- co-branded learning series
- cohort showcases and member demos
- editorial collaborations and published reflections

## What Partners Gain

- a credible entry point into domain-specific AI-agent education
- programming that feels practical rather than hype-driven
- stronger engagement through cohort-based participation
- visible outputs that members can share publicly
