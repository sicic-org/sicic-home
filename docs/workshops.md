# Workshops

## Level-Based Workshop System

Our workshops are organized by level, so each batch belongs to a clear track.
This makes it easier for members to understand where to start, what depth to expect, and who they will be learning alongside.

## Membership Requirement

Completing and passing a **intro-level workshop** is the requirement for acquiring Super Individuals CIC membership.
The workshop is the gateway. Membership is the longer-term community layer that follows.
