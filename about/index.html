About¶

What Super Individuals CIC Is¶

Super Individuals CIC is a workshop and membership community focused on building with AI.

We bring together professionals, builders, and curious minds to explore and apply AI in real-world scenarios, then give them a longer-term community in which to keep building.

What We Do¶

  • workshops
  • cohort-based learning
  • invitation-only membership
  • project building

Our Goal¶

To help individuals become AI-native builders, not just consumers of knowledge.

We do that through a workshop gateway, followed by a deeper membership layer for people who are invited to continue.
